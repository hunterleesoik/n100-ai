08 — Why Not ChatGPT?

AI is the engine. Not the moat.

Every investor asks. Here's why general-purpose AI can't compete.

Can't Book

Trust ≠ Tokens

Stakes Too High
No one books a $100K trip in a chat box with no fallback.
Memory Compounds
ChatGPT resets. KARNAK's 10th trip uses all 9 before it.
